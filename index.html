About us

A dream, a mission, a commitment

Sukhobas is not just a name—it is a dream, a mission, and a commitment to spreading happiness, love, and hope in society.

Founded in 2019, Sukhobas began as a small initiative driven by a simple belief: every person deserves care, dignity, and support during difficult times. During the COVID-19 pandemic, we stepped forward to help individuals and families who were struggling and in need of assistance. What started as a humble effort soon grew into a community of compassionate people dedicated to making a positive difference.

Today, Sukhobas continues its journey by supporting underprivileged individuals, organizing social welfare activities, and encouraging people to come together for the greater good. We believe that even the smallest act of kindness can create a lasting impact.

Our goal is simple: to bring smiles, spread love, and build a better tomorrow for everyone.

"Together, we can make a difference."
