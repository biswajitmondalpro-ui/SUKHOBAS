Take a group of senior citizens out for Durga Puja

This Durga Puja we're organizing a special, free day out for a few elderly citizens — bringing the festival to those who usually watch it from afar.

❤ Support this goal
How the day unfolds

Our plan

  • 1
    A day within the four days of pujo — we pick one date and gather everyone together.
  • 2
    A short pandal tour to admire the finest pandals around the area.
  • 3
    Adda & stories — unhurried, joyful conversation.
  • 4
    Light refreshments — simple, comfortable food and tea through the day.
  • 5
    A gift of clothing for each elder — a small token of the season.
  • 6
    Safely home together, as one group, with care.
