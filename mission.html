What drives us

Our Mission

Happiness, care and dignity for every person — whatever their age or circumstances.

At Sukhobas, we believe that every person deserves happiness, care, and dignity, regardless of their age or circumstances.

Many elderly people spend their entire lives working hard for their families, sacrificing their own dreams and comforts. They know what festivals are, but often never get the chance to truly enjoy them. They spend their lives caring for others, yet in their later years, many find themselves alone, neglected, or without support.

Our mission is to stand beside these individuals and bring joy, comfort, and companionship into their lives.

For our elders, we aim to:
  • ✓
    Support elderly people who are financially or socially disadvantaged.
  • ✓
    Provide assistance for medicines and basic healthcare needs.
  • ✓
    Help arrange medical consultations and treatments whenever possible.
  • ✓
    Organize small trips, celebrations and recreation to create happy memories.
  • ✓
    Spend quality time with senior citizens, treating them as our own family.
  • ✓
    Offer emotional support and companionship to those who need it most.
For underprivileged children

We believe education is the foundation of a brighter future, so we support children from underprivileged backgrounds with essential learning materials:

School bagsBooks & notebooksPens & pencilsStudy kits & learning resources

Sukhobas is still growing, and our journey has only begun. As we move forward, we will continue to identify new ways to serve society and support those in need. Our future initiatives will be guided by one simple principle:

"Where there is a need, there should be a helping hand."

Together, with the support of kind-hearted people, we hope to bring smiles, hope, and happiness to many more lives.
